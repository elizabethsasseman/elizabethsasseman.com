Problem

One of the most common ways patients use our ecommerce platform is by searching for products they know they need to purchase, based on the prescription given to them by their doctor. Jamal is our persona in this situation. We wanted to find a way to visually present this use case to Walmart in a way that incorporates their color palettes with our technology.

Goal & Solution

To put together a flow of Walmart-branded mockups that represent one of the most common ways patients shop for the products they need. Success for this project would be shown in whether these designs influence Walmart stakeholders in a positive direction for all of us.

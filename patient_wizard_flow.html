Patient Wizard Flow

Note: The Wizard Experience Map was put together by another team member.

View Designs Wizard Experience Map

Problem

We don’t currently have a great method for new patients who need help figuring out what products to buy. And in partnering with Walmart for a whitelabel project, finding a solution to this was important. One of the most common types of users is the patient who doesn’t know where to start, or what to buy when they come to our site. Howie is our persona in this situation. We wanted to find a way to accommodate people like Howie.

Goal & Solution

To put together a hand-holding experience of sorts for patients that aren’t sure where to start in their search for products. Success would be measured by testing this new technology with users who fit Howie's persona.

Notes, Thoughts & Requirements

  • Whitelabeled Mockups
  • Some Level of Hand-Holding Experience
  • Base Content on the Wizard Experience Map
  • Mockups that Depict the "Wizard" Method of Searching for Products
  • Personalized Experience
  • Progress Bar
  • Generate Product Recommendations
  • Ability to Update with Insurance Prices
  • Take into Account Last Purchases

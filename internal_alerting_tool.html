Problem

There is a lack of efficiency in the current alerting system for both ourselves and for AdaptHealth. As part of our partnership, we need to come up with a solution to help customer service (CS) representatives stay up-to-date with issues that arise internally, rather than let these issues get lost in transition, or hang around for too long.

Goal & Solution

Create a system that automatically organizes and categorizes important alerts. An important part of this solution would come from new technology on the back-end.

Notes, Thoughts & Requirements

  • Include a Chart View of Alert Types
  • Ability to Filter by Important Metrics
  • Ability to Search by Patient or Order
  • Time Sensitivities
  • Ability to View & Archive Alerts that have been Dealt with
